FileZilla

Filezilla is a free FTP (File Transfer Protocol) software used for transferring files to a server, which hosts your webpage. Once you've uploaded your files, the index.html page will be available for the world to see. Here's how to get started:

  • Download the latest Filezilla here.
  • Go here and sign in with your UW Net ID.
  • Check off the following options if they are in the Inactive Services section: Student Web Publishing and Dante account.
  • Open FileZilla and enter the following information in the boxes found at the top of the screen:
  • Host: dante.u.washington.edu
  • Username: Your Net ID
  • Password: Your Net ID password
  • Port: 22
  • Click Quickconnect at the top right of the screen.
